Vincent Whittman
American television host and serial killer
Born: March 4 1906, Maryland, U.S.
Died: November 18 1958 (aged 52), New York, U.S.
Occupation: Television host, entertainer
Height: 5'11"
Vincent Whittman was an American television personality known for hosting the live variety program The Vincent Whittman Show from 1950 to 1958. He is considered to be one of the most prolific serial killers in modern history, with at least a dozen estimated victims over roughly 20 years. On 31 January 1979, over 20 years after his death, Whittman was convicted of murdering Robert Sinclair and 15 others within the entertainment industry, which resulted in a nationwide scandal and posthumous criminal charges.
